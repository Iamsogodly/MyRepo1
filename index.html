Campus Life

School Church

Established in 1965

Construction started in 1962 and ended in 1964, and the church was opened in 1965. A fire broke out in 1966, and the church was fixed and reopened in 1967. Then, in 1971, the pastor died of heart disease and a New pastor was appointed. The church has been renovated twice- once in 1978 and the second, in 2000. In 2004, the pastor was replaced again. Today, the church is in full service, but the church will be renovated in November, 2024. The pastor will change once again in 2027-2028 time period.



School Library

Established in 1964

Construction started in 1963 and all shelves and books were shifted in 1964. 2 days later, the library was opened. It has been renovated 3 times - once in 1969, the second, in 1983 and the third, in 2014. The books are adapted (new books are put on stores, existing books' new/reprinted editions are placed and old books are donated) every year. There are 5 sections - Horror, Fiction, Non-Fiction, Knowledge and Atlas.



School Auditorium

Established in 1966

Construction of the auditorium began in 1964. It finished in 1966 and was opened for the school's first annual day. It has been renovated once every 10 years since 1966 (1976, 1986, 1996, 2006, 2016) So far around 100 assemblies and annual days have been conducted at the auditorium. It has a capacity of 1000 people.

What's New

  • New School Canteen

    The food options and responsibilities to consider, canteens must be better organised and more efficient than they have ever been in the past. Online menus with photographs of the meals prepared, and detailed descriptions of their ingredients help both students and parents make the right nutritional choices. Each student or staff member who accesses the canteen could be provided with a School Canteen Account on the school website that keeps track of their previous meal choices and any dietary requirements that they may have. Ordering online by ticking a box and paying for meals online can eliminate all the fuss of money handling, making it safer and less complicated for staff and students alike. Here again, diversity is the key. This online ordering will be available soon.

  • Implemented Online Attendance Management System

    The reasons for an IOAMS (Implemented Online Attendance Management System) are: 1. ACCURACY Humans are prone to error, even if we provide them with supporting computational devices, Automated Attendance Management Systems ensure accurate time records and minimize the inevitable and costly errors with manual data entry. This accurate data thereby helps to provide accurate performance and payroll data. 2. ECONOMICS Have you ever thought that attendance management systems could control costs? It saves money by putting an end to inaccurate time reporting, buddy punching, absenteeism, tardiness, time abuse and overpayment. As a matter of fact, a study done by the Axsium Group found that large organisations save up to ₹150,000 per employeee after implementing automated attendance management systems. 3. PRODUCTIVITY Productivity/Efficiency monitoring and managing attendance manually can be a time-consuming, laborious and expensive affair. It takes time to process paper sheets and time cards, create schedules, authorize leave and overtime, and create payroll manually. Free up precious administration time with an automated system that does it all for you, from keeping track of employee hours to automatically importing information into your payroll system. The time and effort saved combined with data accuracy helps in optimizing the use of resources that lead to increased productivity and improves profits. IOAMS also can do much more like Visibility, Insights, Hassle-free Workflow Management, Flexibility, Integration, Configuration, Real-Time Tracking, Security and Compliance.
